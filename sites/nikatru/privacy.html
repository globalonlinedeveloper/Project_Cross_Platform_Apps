By using our Services you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

1. Information we collect

We rely on trusted third parties to deliver the Services. Depending on the app, these may include: the Apple App Store, Google Play and Microsoft Store (distribution and billing); Razorpay (payment processing on our website); analytics and crash-reporting tools; and, where applicable, advertising networks. We share information only as needed to operate the Services (for example, with the payment processor to complete a purchase), to comply with the law or a valid legal request, or to protect our rights and the safety of users. We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

We keep information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described here or as required by law. You can ask us to delete information associated with you by emailing us (see Contact).

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, email support@nikatru.com.
