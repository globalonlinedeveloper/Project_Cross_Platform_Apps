About

I'm an independent software developer who takes products end-to-end — design, development, testing and store publishing — on my own. My focus is building well-crafted apps and games with Flutter, so one codebase ships natively to iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux and the web.

I work under my studio brand NIKATRU, a Government of India registered MSME established in 2021. Whether it's a productivity tool, a game or a web app, I care about clean design, performance and shipping things that actually reach users across every screen.

With 11+ years of professional software development behind me — spanning enterprise systems and independent products — I now channel that experience entirely into designing, building and shipping under NIKATRU.
